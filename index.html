Welcome to the Viper web installer!

  1. Plug in your ESP to a USB port. We will install the HSD to it.
  2. if you update your device, please dont forget to save your settings at "files" tab before update.
  3. Hit "Install" and select the correct COM port. No device found?
  4. Get HSD installed and connected in less than 3 minutes!
